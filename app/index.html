Emotional Alexa is an emotionally charged chatbot made into an Amazon Alexa skill for a Creative Inquiry Day at Baruch College. Right now, Alexa only responds to certain questions like "Alexa, ask your emotional self if you think I am pretty!" or "Alexa, ask your emotional self why I am feeling sad." Eventually, my goal for Emotional Alexa is for her to be completely reliant on machine learning to generate the questions and responses, after being trained on things like psychological texts and demographic-specific vernacular.


The inspiration for Emotional Alexa came from a realization I had about what emotional support means to me, and what I think it means to other people. There were times in the past that I felt helpless in providing my friends with the support they needed through difficult times. But I soon realized that sometimes people just want to talk and be heard; not everyone wants advice. So I figured Amazon Echo’s Alexa could help me out, using her comforting, monotone voice to hear people out on issues like self-confidence, stress, or general emotional conflict.


I encourage you to check out the website because you'll be able to watch the Alexa skill in action ☺.
