Emotional Alexa is an emotionally charged chatbot made into an Amazon Alexa skill for a Creative Inquiry Day at Baruch College. Right now, Alexa only responds to certain questions like "Alexa, ask your emotional self if you think I am pretty!" or "Alexa, ask your emotional self Why I am feeling sad." Eventually, my goal for Emotional Alexa is for her to be completely reliant on machine learning to generate the question responses, after being trained on things like psychological texts and demographic-specific vernacular.


The inspiration for Emotional Alexa was a feeling I had about what emotional support means to me, and what I think it means to other people. I used to feel like my words lacked impact when my friends were going through times of difficulty in their lives and sought advice. But I soon realized that sometimes people just needed to be heard, and not everyone wants advice. So I figured Amazon Echo’s Alexa could help me out, being there to hear people out on issues like self-confidence, stress, or general emotional conflict.


I encourage you to check out the website because you'll be able to watch the Alexa skill in action ☺.
