Emotional Alexa is an emotionally charged chatbot that I made into an Amazon Alexa skill for a Creative Inquiry Day at Baruch College. Right now, Alexa only responds to certain questions like "Alexa, ask your emotional self if you think I am pretty!" or "Alexa, ask your emotional self why I am feeling sad." Eventually, my goal for Emotional Alexa is for her to be completely reliant on machine learning to generate the questions and responses, after being trained on things like psychological texts and demographic-specific vernacular.


I encourage you to check out the website because you'll be able to watch the Alexa skill in action ☺.
