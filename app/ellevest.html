Ellevest

I held my first engineering positon at Ellevest, where I did full stack development work part time while I completed my CS degree.

My first job in engineering.

Ellevest is a digital investing platform geared towards women where I had my first job in engineering. My time there was awesome. The employees who work there are some of the most intelligent individuals I've had the opportunity to be around. I'll always be thankful for my time there, as I was able to learn so much and prepare myself for the next step in my engineering career.

My work at Ellevest began in the Fall of 2015 as a contractor where my first task was a simple build of their Beta release “Sign Up” page. Over the next year and a half, my work progressed into projects like out a promotional "Galentine's Day Page," the preliminary features of a CMS, Rails API endpoints, and all sorts of fun React components. I also spent a large amouint of time refining and adding to Ellevest's back-office application, gaining more strength in Ruby, Rails, and MYSQL.
