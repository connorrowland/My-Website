WHEN

August 2015 – Present

Luca's Jazz Corner

Luca’s Jazz Corner is a website that I designed in October of 2016 for a restaurant owner, Luca, who wanted to transform his restarant into a fully functional jazz club. This website serves as a place for customers to go and find information, videos, and photos of artists, find out more about Luca’s Jazz Corner, and also figure out when artists are performing. I'm super proud of the way this website came out, and it has served as an effective, impactful asset to the business's success, which is all I could ask for as a designer.

Git/Github Graphics

For a freelance project with Trilogy Educational Services, I was instructed to make graphics to explain various Git processes. Below are the graphics that I made, which are now used as learning materials for a coding bootcamp.

Pride March T-Shirt

Law Firm Site

William Ricigliano, P.C. is a Personal Injury Law Firm in Manhattan I have a special connection to. This is where I had my first job when I was 16 as a summer intern, where I did everything from shredding paper to investigating expert witnesses. When I was just starting out as an engineer/designer in the Fall of 2015, I got the opportunity to redesign their website and create some solid brand foundations for them. The old website design was a bit outdated, so I was happy to give it a much needed fresh start.
