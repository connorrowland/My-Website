Law Firm Website

When

October 2015 – May 2016.

What it is

William Ricigliano, P.C. is a Personal Injury Law Firm in Manhattan I have a special connection to. This is where I had my first job when I was 16 as a summer intern, where I did everything from shredding paper to investigative research on expert witnesses.

What I Did

A lot changed since my work as a summer intern, including my career direction which gravitated away from Law and into Web Engineering/Computer Science. From then to now, I never expected that I'd one day rebuild their website and design their logo, but it was an amazing opportunity to do so.

Skills Used

HTML

CSS

Javascript

JQuery

The Issue

There were many problems with the old website that the new one needed to solve. The first one being that the design of the site didn't reflect the brand that the firm wanted to be recognized for. It was a bit outdated, and didn't have many responsive design and/or user experience considerations. So in order for the site to be used as a marketing tool and adequate representation of the firm, it needed to have a fresh start.

What I Changed

The first thing I wanted to be sure to do was establish the desired brand with a logo and color scheme. This guided me to designing and updating the website. I then decided it was important to not only design for all devices, but also build for many different browsers. William Ricigliano, P.C. has a variety of different clients who may not have the most updated or current versions of their browser or operating system, so ensuring the site would "degrade gracefully" was an important task for me.
