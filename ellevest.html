Ellevest

When

September 2015 – Present.

What it is

Ellevest is a digital investment platform geared towards women and the achievement of their various financial goals. Ellevest’s founder, Sallie Krawcheck, has “a solid reputation as a disrupter in the male-dominate world of finance.” Which is why Sallie and the rest of the Ellevest team are working to change the way women invest. According to Sallie, “It’s time to give women an investing experience built specifically for them. Ellevest will be unlike anything else out there and will empower women to take financial control of their lives.” Ellevest is a company I’m proud to be a part of, as I too have a dedication to doing what I can to ensure the amazing women in my life have the opportunities they deserve.

What I Did

My work at Ellevest began in the Fall of 2015 as a contractor where my first task was a simple build of their Beta release “Sign Up” page. As my work for Ellevest continued, I constructed HTML E-mails (which were not so fun, but taught me a lot), in addition to integrating various article views for Ellevest’s “Resource Center” in Ruby on Rails. Once my work for Ellevest as an Engineering Apprentice for the Summer of 2016 started, I spent a large chunk of my time refining and adding to Ellevest's in-office tools. Doing so allowed me to gain a more solidified understanding of Ruby, Rails, and MYSQL, while also giving me more Back End experience I hadn't had much of before.
