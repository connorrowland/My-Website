Let's Meet!

In the Summer of 2015, me and two others created Let's Meet, an application for finding a central meeting place between two locations. We constructed Let's meet using Backbone.Js, Ruby on Rails, JQuery, Handlebars, and the API of both Yelp and Google Maps.
